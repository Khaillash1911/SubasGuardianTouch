Growing Together

Suba’s Guardian Touch was founded in 2019 by Subathra, a mother of two with a lifelong passion for nurturing and supporting families. Her journey began with a love for caring for her own children, where daily massage sessions became meaningful moments of bonding. Guided and inspired by her aunt, a confinement nanny, Subathra expanded her skills beyond lactation cookies to mastering baby massage and bathing techniques.

What started as a personal practice soon grew into a calling to help other mothers experience the same comfort, connection, and confidence. With a focus on baby massage, maternal wellness, and holistic care, we aim to empower families through knowledge, nurturing, and hands-on guidance. Our mission is simple: to create a safe, supportive space where both mother and child can thrive during the precious early stages of life.

❮ ❯

Our Journey

To strengthen her expertise, Subathra furthered her training in Bali, where she completed intensive courses in baby and maternal massage. Over the course of a month, she immersed herself in hands-on practice, refining her techniques under experienced trainers and learning holistic approaches rooted in both tradition and modern care.

Beyond the technical skills, the experience gave her valuable insights into the emotional and physical well-being of both mother and child, reinforcing her belief that nurturing care goes beyond physical touch.
