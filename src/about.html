Our Journey

The story of how Suba's Guardian Touch came to be and our path to helping countless mothers and babies.

The Beginning

Suba's Guardian Touch was born from founder Sarah Williams' personal experience as a new mother struggling with postpartum recovery while trying to soothe her colicky newborn. After discovering the transformative power of infant massage and receiving her own therapeutic treatments, Sarah recognized a gap in support services for new mothers and their babies.

In 2015, with specialized training in both postnatal and infant massage, Sarah opened the first Suba's Guardian Touch studio with a mission to create a sanctuary where mothers could receive care while learning to nurture their babies through touch.

❮ ❯

Growing Together

As word spread about the benefits of our specialized approach, Suba's Guardian Touch quickly became a beloved resource in the community. By 2018, we had expanded our team to include certified lactation consultants, postnatal doulas, and pediatric massage therapists to provide comprehensive support for new families.

Our growth has always been guided by the needs of our clients. When mothers asked for more educational resources, we developed our signature workshop series, "Nurturing Touch for Life," which has now helped over 1,000 families develop confidence in caring for their babies.
